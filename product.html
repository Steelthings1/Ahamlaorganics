About Moringa Leaf Powder

Moringa oleifera, often called the "Miracle Tree," has been used in Ayurvedic medicine for thousands of years. Our Moringa Leaf Powder is made from the freshest leaves, harvested at peak nutritional potency, and gently dried at low temperatures to preserve all natural enzymes and nutrients.

With 7x the Vitamin C of oranges, 4x the calcium of milk, 4x the Vitamin A of carrots, and 3x the potassium of bananas, Moringa is genuinely one of nature's most complete superfoods.

How to Use Moringa Powder

🥤
Smoothies & Juices

Add 1–2 tsp to your morning smoothie or juice. Pairs well with banana, mango, and pineapple.

🍵
Tea & Warm Drinks

Mix 1 tsp in warm (not boiling) water with lemon and honey for a nourishing morning drink.

🍲
Cooking

Sprinkle 1–2 tsp on soups, curries, or dals. Add near the end of cooking to preserve nutrients.

⏰
Recommended Dosage

Start with 1/2 tsp daily and gradually increase to 1–2 tsp per day. Best taken in the morning.
