Our Organic Amla Powder is made from 100% pure Indian Gooseberry (Phyllanthus emblica), sustainably harvested from certified organic farms in the Himalayan foothills. Each batch is cold-pressed at temperatures below 40°C to preserve the full spectrum of bioactive compounds.

Amla contains more Vitamin C than oranges — roughly 600mg per 100g — making it one of nature's most potent antioxidant superfoods. Regular consumption has been linked to improved skin elasticity, stronger hair, enhanced immunity, and better digestive health.
