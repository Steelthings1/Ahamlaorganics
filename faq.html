Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999. Orders below ₹999 are charged a flat ₹60 shipping fee. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for ₹120, and overnight delivery for ₹200 regardless of order value.

  • Express Delivery: 1–2 business days
  • Overnight Delivery: Next business day

Delays are rare but can occasionally happen due to weather events, public holidays, or high demand periods. If your order hasn't arrived within the estimated delivery window, please contact our support team with your order ID and we'll investigate immediately. If the delay is on our end, we'll offer you a delivery upgrade or discount on your next order.

All orders are shipped in plain brown corrugated boxes with no external branding. The only label is the delivery address and our company name "Ahamla Organics." Inside, your products are individually sealed in our eco-friendly, airtight resealable bags with full product labelling.
