How We Started

A Kitchen Experiment That Changed Everything

It all began in 2019 when our founder, Hilal Ahmed, was struggling to find genuinely pure organic powders for his family. Every product on the market was either adulterated, overpriced, or came with vague sourcing claims.

Frustrated and determined, Hilal connected directly with small organic farms across India — from the moringa groves of Tamil Nadu to the ashwagandha fields of Rajasthan — and began sourcing, processing, and sharing these superfoods with friends and family.

The response was overwhelming. Word spread. What started as a kitchen experiment became Ahamla Organics — a brand built on radical transparency, verified purity, and a deep respect for the farmers who make it all possible.

Today, we work with over 30 certified organic farms, serve thousands of customers across India, and remain committed to the same promise we made on day one: pure, potent, and honest.
